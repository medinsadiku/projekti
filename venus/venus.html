About Venus

Venus, the second planet from the Sun, is a rocky planet similar in size and composition to Earth. Known for its scorching temperatures and thick atmosphere, Venus offers a mysterious and otherworldly experience for travelers looking to explore the beauty and dangers of our neighboring planet.
